# Venezuela Earthquake 2026 — NGO & Stakeholder Map

> **Event:** Twin earthquakes (M7.2 + M7.5), off the northern coast west of Caracas, June 24, 2026.
> Most-affected: La Guaira / coastal corridor + western states. Casualties as reported: ~589 dead,
> ~2,980 injured (figures evolving — verify before publishing).
>
> **Purpose:** A "who does what" reference grouped by functional category, to inform the Hub's
> Resource_Directory and Donation channels. Compiled from public sources (June 2026).
>
> **Legend:**
> - ✅ **In Hub** — already cited in the Hub (`seed.ts` / `config.ts`).
> - 🆕 **Candidate** — verified-looking responder not yet in the Hub; candidate for curation.
> - ⚠️ **Verify** — named in secondary coverage; confirm legitimacy + active engagement before listing.
>
> Nothing here is "verified" in the Hub sense until an Administrator curates it. Treat this as a
> research lead-list, not a published record.

## Gap analysis vs. the Hub today

The Hub currently cites **Caritas, IFRC/Venezuelan Red Cross, UNICEF** (donations) and the
people-finder / damage-map / Yummy tools. The biggest **un-tapped, credible** categories are:

- **Medical:** Direct Relief, International Medical Corps, Healing Venezuela.
- **Food/Water/Shelter:** World Central Kitchen, WFP, Convoy of Hope.
- **Children/Protection:** Save the Children, RET International.
- **Aggregators:** GlobalGiving relief fund (lets the Hub point donors at vetted-local pooling).
- **Coordination context:** OCHA / PAHO / clusters — useful as *informational* Resource_Directory
  entries (what the official response structure is), not donation channels.
